Personalized Weight Gain, Muscle Building & Skin Care Plan

Nutrition Plan

  • Morning (7-8 AM): 4 boiled eggs or omelette, 2 whole wheat bread slices or paratha with ghee, 1 glass milk with soaked almonds & raisins
  • Mid-Morning Snack (10-11 AM): Banana + peanut butter smoothie, Handful of walnuts or dry fruits
  • Lunch (1-2 PM): 2-3 rotis or rice, Paneer/chicken/fish, Dal + veggies, Curd
  • Evening Snack (5-6 PM): Protein shake or boiled eggs, Roasted chana, peanuts, or sprouts
  • Dinner (8-9 PM): Rice + chicken/fish curry or dal, Veggies, Salad with olive oil or flaxseeds
  • Before Bed: 1 glass warm milk with turmeric, 1 banana or soaked almonds

Workout Plan (6 Days a Week)

  • Day 1: Chest + Triceps
  • Day 2: Back + Biceps
  • Day 3: Legs
  • Day 4: Shoulders + Core
  • Day 5: Full-body + Cardio
  • Day 6: Rest or light stretching

Tip: Start with bodyweight exercises and progress to weights.

Skin Health & Care Tips

  • Drink 3-4 liters of water daily
  • Eat Vitamin C-rich fruits (orange, amla, papaya)
  • Use sunscreen daily to avoid darkening
  • Face mask: Multani mitti + rose water (2-3x/week)
  • Sleep 7-8 hours

Optional Supplements (check with doctor): Omega-3, Multivitamin with zinc & Vitamin E
